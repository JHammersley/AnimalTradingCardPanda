Giant Panda

Unlike other bears Panda's eyes are more akin to a cats eyes and have vertical slits for pupils.

  • Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Life Span: 20 years
  • Average Weight: 150 - 280 lbs
  • Diet: Bamboo

Giant Pandas are an endangered species that live high in dense bamboo forests of China. They spend most their time eating, anywhere from 12 to 18 hours of eating mainly bamboo. Bamboo makes up 99% of their diet and they eat 26 to 84 pounds of it per day.
